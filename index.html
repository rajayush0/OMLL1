Marine Drive + Church

25 December — the day I felt you like never before

"The most unforgettable evenings are the ones where the little moments make you fall in love all over again. This one was made of soft light, shy laughter, and the warmth of your hand in mine."

In the church, I watched you struggle with the candle flame. When I reached out to help, every nervous smile and thankful look felt like a confession. That quiet moment made my heart louder than the bells.

Later, at Marine Drive with tea warming our hands, the city lights sparkled and I felt something gentle and real bloom inside me. I knew then that this feeling was more than a fancy — it was love, slow and sure.

🌙 Thank you for letting me be the one to help you, to sit close, and to fall deeper with you.
